[*] LASK and FC Linz are to merge into LASK Linz at the end of the season;

Note: FC Admira/Wacker changed name to SC Niederösterreich FC Admira/Wacker.
Grazer AK played most home matches in the Alpen-Stadion of Kapfenberg, while
their own Casino-Stadion was being renovated.  FC Linz and LASK Linz (new
name for Linzer ASK) played the first few matches of the season in the
Union-Stadion of Wels.  SCN FC Admira/Wacker played several matches in
Krems and Sankt Pölten in a vain effort to gain a new identity as the
club of the "Land" (province) Niederösterreich.
